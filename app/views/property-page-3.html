Description

The property comprises a converted 19th century former convent school in the centre of Bath and occupies a prominent position on a corner plot. It is used as a local administrative centre with ground floor interview rooms.

The vacant space comprises the entire third floor which is contained within a tiled timber roof with dorma windows. The windows are steel casement and on the lower floors are housed within stone reveals and mullions.

There is gas central heating throughout and all floors are serviced by a lift. Then property is surrounded by a mature garden to the front and sides and there is a pay and display car park .
